Learnings

In my learning journey, I'm not just stopping at Virtual Machines, but also getting hands-on experience with tools like Ansible and Docker. Ansible is helping me automate the setup and management of various services, making things more efficient. Docker, on the other hand, continues to impress me with its lightweight containerization, ensuring that the services I work on use resources wisely. And there's more! I'm delving into the realm of CyberOps, where I'm learning about cybersecurity operations. It's fascinating to understand how to secure systems, detect potential threats, and respond effectively to keep everything running smoothly. With these added layers of knowledge, I'm eagerly anticipating the challenges and opportunities that the third semester of my elective course will bring. Exploring advanced concepts, hands-on experiences, and the ever-evolving world of technology. I'm also learning a lot about Virtual Machines, where I get to set up and manage important business services like Apache and MariaDB. Plus, I'm diving into monitoring tools like Nagios and Prometheus. Docker is also on the menu, and it's cool because it runs containers using fewer resources than regular virtual machines. My professor is awesome and has been teaching us about all these different tools and services. Now, I'm excited to move on to the third semester of my elective course, where I'll explore even more advanced stuff and get hands-on with new technologies. It's shaping up to be a really interesting journey!
