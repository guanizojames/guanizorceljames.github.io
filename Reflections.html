HI It's me R-cel James D. Guanizo your Future Computer Engineering

In this elective track, the hands-on experience of downloading and configuring virtual machines has been nothing short of wonderful. Thanks to our dedicated professor, who not only introduced us to the intricacies of virtual machines but also walked us through the essentials. Setting up and managing different virtual machines has provided a practical understanding of how these systems work. As we delved into the realm of enterprise services like Apache, MariaDB, and monitoring tools such as Nagios and Prometheus, the learning curve became even more exciting. The addition of Ansible and Docker to the mix brought automation and containerization into the picture, showing me new ways to streamline processes and enhance efficiency. One aspect that has truly sparked my interest is the exploration of CyberOps. Understanding the fundamentals of cybersecurity operations has added a critical layer to my skill set. Learning how to secure systems and respond effectively to potential threats has been both challenging and rewarding. With the upcoming years to come that i have left in this academe, I'm enthusiastic about delving deeper into advanced concepts and technologies. The prospect of further refining my skills with Ansible, Docker, and CyberOps, along with the guidance of our knowledgeable professor, is making the upcoming semester something to look forward to. The journey continues, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.
